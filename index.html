About Red Threads

Concept & Interaction Design

"Red Threads" is an interactive exploration of destiny, connection, and the generative nature of human relationships. Inspired by the Eastern myth of the Red Thread of Fate, this project reimagines connections not as static bonds, but as living, breathing algorithms that tangle, stretch, and evolve.
